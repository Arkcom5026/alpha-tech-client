The Delivery Note issue revealed an important operational truth mismatch. — ปัญหาใบส่งของเปิดเผยความไม่สอดคล้องของ operational truth ที่สำคัญ

P1 Document Line / Receipt Decision Update

Discovery:
Receipt can originate from two flows:
1) Cash Sale → Receipt directly
2) Credit Sale → Delivery Note first → Receipt later

Decision:

Temporary Patch Rule:
For now, Receipt Short may use existing SaleItem / SaleItemSimple fields:
- documentPrefix
- documentDescription
- documentSuffix

This enables immediate edit support for receipt lines with low backend impact.

Known Limitation:
During temporary patch phase, Delivery Note and Receipt still share the same SaleItem document fields. Therefore editing receipt document text may affect Delivery Note display for the same Sale.

Future Architecture Lock:
When returning for the larger P1 refactor, create document-type-specific truth:
- DeliveryNoteDocumentLine
- ReceiptDocumentLine
- TaxInvoiceDocumentLine
or a generic DocumentLine table with documentType.

Future model must support:
- Receipt from cash sale
- Receipt from one delivery note
- Receipt from multiple delivery notes / sales
- Initial copy/inherit from source document lines
- Independent override after receipt creation
- Traceability back to source SaleItem / SaleItemSimple

# UB — P1 Product Runtime Classification & Intake Architecture

**Status:** LOCKED (Blueprint Candidate)

## Mission

Define the canonical product classification hierarchy for P1 and establish the runtime contract used by Product Intake, Inventory, POS, and Marketplace Discovery.

---

## Human Reality

Discovery from rebuilding Product Runtime after a major inventory reset.

During recovery it became clear that product classification must serve two different responsibilities:

1. Store operational runtime.
2. Hyperlocal marketplace discovery.

These responsibilities must remain connected but should not be merged.

---

## Canonical Product Hierarchy

```
Category
    ↓
GlobalProductType
    ↓
ProductType
    ↓
ProductTypeBrand
    ↓
Brand
    ↓
Product
```

---

## Runtime Responsibilities

### Category

Highest-level business grouping.

Used for broad business organization.

Not intended for day-to-day inventory operation.

---

### GlobalProductType

Marketplace Discovery Layer.

Primary responsibility:

* Online Search
* Hyperlocal Discovery
* Marketplace Recommendation
* Cross-store Product Grouping

This layer exists primarily for the platform rather than for store operators.

Store users should rarely interact with it directly.

---

### ProductType

Operational Runtime Classification.

Used by:

* POS
* Inventory
* Purchasing
* Product Intake
* Reporting

This is the product type that store operators actually understand.

Example:

Printer

Notebook

Monitor

Ink Tank

Router

---

### ProductTypeBrand

Allowed Brand Matrix.

This table is not merely a join table.

It defines which brands are valid for each ProductType.

Example:

Printer

* Canon
* Epson
* Brother
* HP

Notebook

* Dell
* Lenovo
* ASUS
* MSI

The runtime should filter Brand selections using ProductTypeBrand.

Store users should never browse every brand in the system.

---

### Brand

Manufacturer identity.

Brand selection should always be constrained by ProductTypeBrand whenever possible.

---

### Product

Runtime Truth of sellable inventory.

Product represents the operational truth used by:

* Stock
* Sales
* Pricing
* Barcode
* Inventory
* Runtime Documents

Product is never responsible for marketplace discovery.

---

## Product Intake Runtime

Recommended operational flow:

```
Choose ProductType
        ↓
Load ProductTypeBrand
        ↓
Choose Brand
        ↓
Create / Select Product
        ↓
BranchPrice
        ↓
StockItem / SimpleLot
        ↓
StockBalance
        ↓
StockMovement
```

The runtime should minimize user decisions while preserving operational correctness.

---

## SQL Recovery Strategy

Before Product Intake begins:

Prepare Product Master using SQL.

Recommended preparation order:

```
Category
↓
GlobalProductType
↓
ProductType
↓
Brand
↓
ProductTypeBrand
↓
Product
```

Frontend Product Intake should rely on this prepared master data rather than requiring operators to enter everything manually.

---

## UX Principle

Operational users should interact with:

* ProductType
* Brand
* Product

The following layers should remain mostly invisible:

* Category
* GlobalProductType
* ProductTypeBrand

The system should guide users toward valid choices instead of requiring them to understand the internal architecture.

---

## Locked Understanding

ProductTypeBrand is an operational validation layer.

GlobalProductType is a marketplace discovery layer.

ProductType is the operational runtime layer.

Product is the runtime truth for inventory and sales.

This separation allows P1 to support both real-world store operations and future hyperlocal commerce discovery without mixing responsibilities.

UB — P1 Recovery Catalog Completion & Product Master Stabilization

Status: LOCKED ✅

Mission

Restore the Product Catalog to an operationally usable state before attempting inventory reconstruction.

ยกระดับ Product Catalog ให้พร้อมใช้งานจริงก่อนเริ่มการกู้คืน Stock Runtime

Human Reality

During production recovery it became clear that store operation cannot resume if Product Master is incomplete.

จากการกู้ระบบจริงพบว่า หาก Product Master ไม่สมบูรณ์ ร้านจะไม่สามารถกลับมาใช้งานได้ แม้โครงสร้าง Stock จะยังอยู่ก็ตาม

Recovery Strategy

Recovery follows a staged approach.

Product
        ↓
BranchPrice
        ↓
StockItem
        ↓
StockMovement
        ↓
StockBalance

Product Master must be restored before inventory truth.

Operational catalog recovery takes priority over stock reconstruction.

Recovery Principle

Do not generate empty inventory merely to satisfy database completeness.

อย่าสร้าง StockItem เปล่าเพียงเพื่อให้ฐานข้อมูลครบ

Inventory should be restored only when operationally necessary.

Stock Runtime should reflect real inventory rather than assumed inventory.

UB — Runtime UI Simplification (LOCKED ✅)

Principle

Runtime UI ต้องแสดงเฉพาะข้อมูลที่ผู้ปฏิบัติงานจำเป็นต้องตัดสินใจเท่านั้น

Decision

ยกเลิกการเลือก ประเภทการรับเข้า (Transaction Type) ใน Stock Intake Runtime
ระบบกำหนดที่มาของรายการ (Source) จากบริบทของการทำงานโดยอัตโนมัติ
ผู้ใช้มีหน้าที่เพียงเลือกสินค้า กำหนดราคา และรับสินค้าเข้า

ผมคิดว่านี่สอดคล้องกับแนวคิดที่เรายึดมาตลอดคือ

"ลดการตัดสินใจของผู้ใช้ให้เหลือเฉพาะสิ่งที่จำเป็นต่อการปฏิบัติงาน"

ซึ่งจะทำให้หน้าจอนี้เรียบง่าย ใช้งานเร็ว และลดโอกาสเลือกค่าผิดโดยไม่จำเป็นครับ
